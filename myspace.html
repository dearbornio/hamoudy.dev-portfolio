About Me

My first computer was a 300MHz IBM with a monitor that sat atop. It came with Windows 98 and brand new on the market 56k, the fastest internet speeds ever! The world was at my fingertips. I could learn whatever I wanted and I didn't need to leave the house. I soon upgraded it to Windows 2000, updated the graphics cards, the sound card multiple times, and a 32x CD-RW!

I taught myself to program with Visual Basic, I made websites with Frontpage, taught myself how to hack other computers, and I was even a beta tester for BitTorrent. The early 2000s was a great time to be the internet.

I have 20+ years of experience with website design and at least another 20 years of experience with hardware/software support. Naturally I run a tech company, dearborn.io, a Website as a Service (WaaS) company, with additional business services coming soon.

I decided to make this page as a "developer profile" and went with the iconic MySpace theme of the early 2000s, music player included. Deciding on a song is still one of the most difficult decisions for this page.
